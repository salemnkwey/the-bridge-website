**Badge** — a compact pill for status, category, or level labels. Use `tone="accent"` solid for "Gratuit", `tone="navy"` for CEFR levels, `tone="teal"` for category, `tone="danger"` solid with a dot for "Live".

```jsx
<Badge tone="accent" variant="solid">Gratuit</Badge>
<Badge tone="navy">Niveau B1</Badge>
<Badge tone="teal" icon="sparkles">Nouveau</Badge>
```

- `soft` = tinted background (default); `solid` = filled. Keep labels to 1–2 words.
